# ReviewQR AI 🚀

AI-powered Google Review QR SaaS platform for restaurants and local businesses.

Customers scan a QR code, answer quick feedback questions, and receive AI-generated Google reviews within seconds.

Built to help businesses increase Google reviews, improve Google Maps visibility, and attract more customers.

# 📱 How It Works

## Business Owner
1. Sign up
2. Add business details
3. Generate QR code
4. Place QR on tables/counter
5. Track analytics

---

## Customer
1. Scan QR code
2. Give quick ratings
3. AI generates review instantly
4. Copy review
5. Submit on Google

---

# 💡 Problem Solved

Most customers do not leave reviews because:
- writing reviews takes effort
- users are lazy to type
- feedback flow is slow

ReviewQR AI reduces friction and helps businesses collect more reviews effortlessly.
